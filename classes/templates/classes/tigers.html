Frequently Asked Questions

Our classes take place in a matted Dojang. Takewondo is done with bare feet so there's no need to buy expensive trainers.We suggest that all new starters wear loose fitting clothing (without zips or buttons) until you have obtained your Dobok (provided with your membership).

Additional items can be purchased from the Coaches. We also suggest that students come with a bottle of water and a if preferred a towel.

All students require a licence and insurance. We have placed these in the annual membership to Teifi Taekwondo.

Sparring equipment can be purchased at any point in during thier training journey, however due to the nature of the classes for our Tigers this isn't as necessary as older classes.

Sparring equipment consists of head guard, gloves, feet protection, shin pads, gum shield, and either a groin guard (Boys & Men) or breast protectors (ladies). Due to the volume of equipment a bag to hold them in is suggested.

Grading for belts is purely optional. The belt system sets goals for students and encourages them to learn new things and face fresh challenges. However, if a student does not wish to grade, we will still be happy for them to train with us.

Our Tigers syllabus means that unlike moving throughbelts swiftly,children will progress in smaller meaningful chunks and therefore will not earn a new coloured belt at every grading
