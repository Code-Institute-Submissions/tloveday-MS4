Tigers Taekwondo

Class Information
Tuesday & Thursday 5:00:pm - 6:00pm 4 - 6

Our Tigers programme has been specifically created to be interactive and engaging for our youngest of Martial Artists. Young children are curious and active, they sometimes have difficulty learning to control their bodies and their behaviour. They crave structure and thrive in an environment that is supportive, educational, and fun.

Our Tiger's learn through a variety of games and activities that introduce the techniques and skills which will set them up for our older classes. The structure of our classes shows children the importance of focus and teaches them to apply it to easily achievable tasks.

The programme designed for the Tigers goes beyond the teaching of Martial Arts and self-defence to include the teaching of self-protection, important life skills and character education. They learn to control their movements to the best of their ability. They also practice hand-eye coordination and learn about their bodies.

Every parent’s paramount concern is their children’s safety, so we include safety orientated lessons that focus on topics such as dealing with bullies and strangers.

We never forget… kids are kids, and they deserve to have some fun! We can’t guarantee that their normal school teachers are making learning an enjoyable experience, but we DO guarantee that your kid will have a blast in our martial arts classes.

Come and see for yourself and book free lessons to try out taekwondo with us.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our classes take place in a matted Dojang. Takewondo is done with bare feet so there's no need to buy expensive trainers.We suggest that all new starters wear loose fitting clothing (without zips or buttons) until you have obtained your Dobok (provided with your membership).

Additional items can be purchased from the Coaches. We also suggest that students come with a bottle of water and a if preferred a towel.

All students require a licence and insurance. We have placed these in the annual membership to Teifi Taekwondo.

Sparring equipment can be purchased at any point in during thier training journey, however due to the nature of the classes for our Tigers this isn't as necessary as older classes.

Sparring equipment consists of head guard, gloves, feet protection, shin pads, gum shield, and either a groin guard (Boys & Men) or breast protectors (ladies). Due to the volume of equipment a bag to hold them in is suggested.

Grading for belts is purely optional. The belt system sets goals for students and encourages them to learn new things and face fresh challenges. However, if a student does not wish to grade, we will still be happy for them to train with us.

Our Tigers syllabus means that unlike moving throughbelts swiftly,children will progress in smaller meaningful chunks and therefore will not earn a new coloured belt at every grading

Parents will be informed of thier Tigers upcoming gradings by their instructor when the time comes
