Juniors Taekwondo

Class Information
Tuesday & Thursday 6:15pm - 7:15pm 7 - 13

Our junior classes are perfect for children aged 6 – 13 years old. They will have fun and meet other children with similar interests, whilst burning away that abundance of energy before bed time. Between the foods we eat and the constant sitting and staring into screens and devices, many kids are not getting enough regular exercise.

Our martial arts classes for kids are full exercise routines disguised as a boatload of fun! That is because they are jam packed with activities, games and drills that are all designed to disguise the repetition of training. Children who are new to taekwondo will be taught the basics like how to block, strike, punch and kick. Then they work through the syllabus with our instructors, they will be taught more advanced techniques and patterns to progress.

Every parent’s paramount concern is their children’s safety, so we include safety orientated lessons that focus on topics such as dealing with bullies and strangers.

We never want our kids to have to use these skills in modern life, but every good parent still wants their kids to be safe. That’s why our martial arts training is very serious about teaching your kids the skills needed for self-protection in any circumstance, from the neighbourhood bully to a stranger on the street (and so much more). But knowing how to defend oneself is only half the battle. Our classes teach your child the respect and self-discipline needed to diffuse a situation without violence whenever possible – as well as the knowledge to know when force is the only way to safety.

We never forget… kids are kids, and they deserve to have some fun! We can’t guarantee that their normal school teachers are making learning an enjoyable experience, but we DO guarantee that your kid will have a blast in our martial arts classes.

Come and see for yourself and book free lessons to try out taekwondo with us.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our classes take place in a matted Dojang. Takewondo is done with bare feet so there's no need to buy expensive trainers. We suggest that all new starters wear loose fitting clothing (without zips or buttons) until you have obtained your Dobok (provided with your membership).

Additional items can be purchased from the shop. We also suggest that students come with a bottle of water and a if preferred a towel.

All students require a licence and insurance. We have placed these in the annual membership to Teifi Taekwondo.

This really depends on how old and mature the child is. The honest answer is, we prefer for parents to stay and watches. We have found that if parents are not present its very difficult for them to understand what the child has to learn to achieve the next belt, it also makes the getting sparring equipment on and off a little easier.

Sparring equipment can be purchased at any point in during your training journey, however ALL students will need sparring equipment when they are promoted to Green Belt Blue Stripe. We like to use sparring in various disguising during training

Your sparring equipment consists of head guard, gloves, foot guards, shin pads, gum shield, and either a groin guard (Boys & Men) or breast protectors (ladies). Due to the volume of equipment a bag to hold them in is suggested.

Grading for belts is purely optional. The belt system sets goals for students and encourages them to learn new things and face fresh challenges. However, if a student does not wish to grade, we will still be happy for them to train with us.

There are 4 coloured belt gradings a year. Every March, June, September and December.

There are 2 black belt gradings a year. Every April and October

Juniors will be invited to participate in gradings by thier instructor when the time comes
